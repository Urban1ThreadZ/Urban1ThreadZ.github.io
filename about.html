FAQ

What types of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and PayPal. Payment must be made in full before the product is released for pickup or delivery.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Orders are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Pickup orders can be picked up at our location during business hours. Delivery orders typically take 3-5 business days to arrive after processing.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase. After 24 hours, orders are non-refundable.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

We do not offer returns or exchanges unless the product is defective. If you receive a defective product, please contact us within 7 days of receipt for assistance.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order using the tracking number provided in the shipping confirmation email.
